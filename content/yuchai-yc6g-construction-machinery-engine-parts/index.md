Cooling for Harsh Climates</h2><p >Construction sites in the Middle East, North Africa and northern Brazil routinely exceed 45 °C ambient. The YC6G's cooling package must be kept aggressive: clean the radiator core daily in dusty conditions, verify fan-clutch operation, and use a 50/50 ethylene-glycol mix with SCA. Operators who skip radiator cleaning see head-gasket failures within a single hot season — the single most expensive avoidable repair.</p><h2 >7.
