The YCK Platform: Yuchai's Next-Generation Architecture</h2><p >The YCK series is built on a modular, high-pressure common-rail (HPCR) platform that shares core design features across all displacement variants. Key architectural elements include:</p><ul ><li ><strong >High-pressure common-rail fuel system</strong> — 1,800–2,500 bar injection pressure, multi-injection events per cycle for optimized combustion and reduced emissions</li><li ><strong >EGR + SCR aftertreatment</strong> — Cooled exhaust gas recirculation combined with selective catalytic reduction for China VI / Euro VI compliance</li><li ><strong >Four-valve-per-cylinder cylinder head</strong> — Improved volumetric efficiency compared to the two-valve designs on legacy YC6 platforms</li><li ><strong >Rear gear train</strong> — Reduced noise and improved timing precision versus front-mounted gear trains</li><li ><strong >Integrated engine brake (YCK11/YCK13)</strong> — Jacobs-type compression release brake for heavy-duty truck applications</li><li ><strong >B10 life of 1,500,000 km+</strong> (truck variants) — Significantly improved durability versus the 800,000 km B10 life of the YC6L platform</li></ul><div  class="note"><strong >Platform transition note for buyers:</strong> The YCK series is progressively replacing the YC6M (mechanical pump), YC6L (early common-rail) and YC6K (interim platform) engines across Yuchai's heavy-duty product line. Parts for these legacy platforms will remain available for years, but fleets planning long-term engine procurement should budget for YCK aftertreatment components (DOC, DPF, SCR catalyst, AdBlue/DEF dosing system) which add significant operating cost compared to China IV/V engines.
