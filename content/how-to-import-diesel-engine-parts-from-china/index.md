For overhaul kits and regular consumables, sea LCL is the sweet spot on speed vs cost.</p></div></div><div  class="step"><div  class="step-num">6</div><div  class="step-body"><strong >Handle Documentation & Customs</strong><p >Required docs: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading/Air Waybill, Certificate of Origin.
